About Us

Kural foundation is a startup under SHRI – DST with a multi-disciplinary team working to elevate the originators of Tamilnadu by supporting and designing with them on the field. Kural has its roots extended not only to the design field but also onto the technical domain by establishing Dhakshakalam – Artisan's prototype development center with facilities such as casting center, 3D scanning & 3d printing lab and AR/VR Lab

Kural school of design has grown with the vision of tacit knowledge transfer between craftsman and designers for a better artistic society. With the informal teaching for 2 years, kural team started the school of design which has its motto to kindle the designer in everyone. Kural school of design assures to walk with students on every step to guide them and be supportive in both technical and design aspects.

With best tutors to lead the students on a right path and state of the art facilities to support students on all technical level, Kural school of design is on its designated stride in bringing out the creative designers with strong knowledge on fundamentals in designing.

Why Design Thinking is Needed?

“A problem well stated is a problem half solved”. This is a powerful yet simple paradigm applicable in every field. Design thinking enables you to do ‘more with less’. It trains your mind to arrive at your answers in a systematic and structured approach.

Who Should Attend?

With no boundaries to imagination, any graduate – architect, designer or an engineer - desiring to create and yearning to invent, befits the course.

FAQ

To understand the concept of design thinking and product development, students must have a preliminary knowledge on how day-to-day products works. This course will help you in applying the fundamentals on real day products. This course may act as a bridge for graduates to reach better career opportunities with the skills learnt in course period. This course also aims to train the students with an independent business mind.

Mastering the creative thinking mind is a main motive of the course. Graduates of this course will emerge as an expertise in prototype development with management and financial handling skills. With the exposure towards 3D scanning, modelling and printing students will get an overall knowledge on rapid prototyping and materialization. Students will also gain more knowledge on social & context-based design principle. UX / UI design will also be a added advantage on their career path.
