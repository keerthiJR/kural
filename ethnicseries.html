Acoustic Products

Ethnic Solution for a modern platform


Acoustic Panels

Pathamdai mats can be used as a absorptive wall panels. It controls echo and reverberation in space and enhances the speech intelligibility.

Acoustic Clouds

This stylish piece of mat can be suspended from the ceiling, which helps to absorb residual sounds and enhances the sound quality of the space.

Mat Collections

Design varieties and collections


Nanaal

Naanal showcases a rich colour range of black, orange and chocolate designed in tie and dye technique by the weavers. Featured tri-colours balances gracefully with 7 kambu traditional pattern. The classic hand woven mat is a fusion of contemporariness and rich ethnicity.

Tribal

This mat is a creative expression of the term ‘Tribal’. The colours and the geometric patterns used represents the culture and traditions of the tribes.

Kolam

This stunning mat is inspired from traditional decorative art ‘kolam’ drawn in the entrances of our homes. It has a wide range of russet colours. This fine quality mat has combinations of various traditional patterns brings out a modern style mat.
